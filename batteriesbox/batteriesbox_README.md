# Batteries box
Box to hold your batteries. Available as a big and small box for both AA and AAA batteries.

Small boxes hold 11 (AAA) and 6 (AA) batteries. Big boxes hold 20 (AAA) and 12 (AA) batteries.
Boxes can be stacked (two small on one big possible).
